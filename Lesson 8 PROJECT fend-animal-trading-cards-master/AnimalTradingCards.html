Siberian Husky

The color of a Husky's nose depends on the color of its fur.

  • Origin: Siberia, Russia
  • Color: All colors from black to white along with a brown/red color
  • Litter size: 4-8 puppies
  • Lifespan: 12-14 years

The Siberian Husky is recognizable by its thickly furred double coat, erect triangular ears, and distinctive markings.
